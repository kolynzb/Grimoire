## Apps included in Grimoire

Grimoire currently consists of 3 applications or sub-repos.

1. [`design`](https://github.com/kolynzb/Grimoire/tree/main/apps/design) - contains information about our ui/ux design and how to make contributions to it.
2. [`site`](https://github.com/kolynzb/Grimoire/tree/main/apps/site) - contains our [landing page](http://gri-moire.vercel.app/) built in [next js](https://nextjs.org/) , [saas-ui](https://saas-ui.dev/)
3. [`docs`](https://github.com/kolynzb/Grimoire/tree/main/apps/docs) - contains our overall [project documentation](http://gri-moire.vercel.app/) built with [nextra](https://nextra.site).
